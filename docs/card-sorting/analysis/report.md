# Card Sorting Analysis – BYU CS Classes

Key Patterns

- Plan My Degree: Degree requirements, 4-year plans, graduation checklist, minor, prerequisite chains.
- Register for Classes: Class schedules/sections, registration how-to, add/drop, waitlists, prerequisite override.
- Course Info: Course pages, syllabi, textbooks, Canvas links, course evaluations.
- Help & Advising: TA hours, tutoring, office hours, advising appointments, contact advising, FAQs.
- Policies & Forms: Academic integrity, repeating courses, grade appeals, petitions/forms, code of conduct.
- Opportunities & Careers: Internship credit, capstone, undergrad research, clubs/events, jobs/internships, scholarships, career services, alumni.
- Resources: Labs/locations, computing/VPN, software licenses, accessibility, international students.
- People: Faculty directory, staff directory, contact us.

Frequent labels students used

- “Plan My Degree” or “Degree Planning”
- “Register”/“Register for Classes” (which is clearer than “Enrollment”).
- “Course Info”/“Course Information” for per-course materials and links.
- “Help & Advising” was usually combined; some split Help (TAs/Tutoring) vs Advising.

Splits

- TA hours vs Tutoring vs Office hours: sometimes was combined/split; think a single help hub with subpages.
- Canvas links: sometimes under Course Info, sometimes under Resources; prefer per-course placement.
- Course evaluations: low-use but expected under Course Info.
- Prerequisite chains: split between Plan and Course Info; recommend showing both in plan hub and on each course page.

Proposed IA Changes

- Navigation: Plan My Degree • Register • Courses • Help & Advising • Policies & Forms • Opportunities & Careers • Resources • People.
- Groupings: Align content to the clusters above; move career services under Opportunities & Careers.
- Cross reference search tool: Add a “What class should I take next?” tool in Plan

Priority Recommendations

- P1: Introduce a dedicated “Plan My Degree” hub with degree reqs, 4-year plans, and prereq chains.
- P2: Consolidate TA hours, Tutoring, and Office hours under “Help & Advising,” with quick search.
- P1: Update top navigation to the eight-item structure above.
- P7: Standardize course page template to include prereqs, textbooks, Canvas link, and syllabi.
- P8: Create a focused “Register for Classes” area with deadlines, add/drop, waitlists, and overrides.
- P4: Add a short glossary/FAQ to reduce confusion around Policies vs Forms.

Appendix (brief)

- Consistency: about 75% of cards placed consistently across ≥60% of participants.
- Interesting quote: “I think in terms of ‘Plan → Register → Do class’.”
